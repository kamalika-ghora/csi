What is Zephyr


Zephyr is the annual technical festival of Thakur College of Engineering and Technology. It started back in 2004 with the objective of providing students a platform to enhance and exhibit their technical expertise. Over the course of years since its inception, not only has there been a swingeing increase in the footfall, but the events have also been broadened to include absolutely fun and thrilling gaming events and tournaments. Zephyr is a 3-day grand festival that is attended by individuals from all walks of life including students, academicians as well as the general public. Zephyr provides everyone with a platform where they can find the things that they enjoy doing the most.

Do you want to geek out or learn something new to impress anyone with technical finesse? Do you portray yourself as a gamer and want to compete against other people? Maybe you just want to meet new people and make great memories together? Or you probably just want to do it all. If that's what you're looking for, Zephyr is the perfect destination for you. What would be more fun than to break free from your everyday routine and participate in something that you love? Come join us for our annual tech fest - Zephyr 2021-22. Keep your eyes peeled for a lot of fun! See you there!
